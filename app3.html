What is Labeled Core?

Lagos State Labeled Core is an identification system used to organize neighborhoods under their respective Local Government Areas (LGAs), supporting governance, emergency response, and public communication.

How It Works

Select any Local Government Area below and tap the call button. Your phone will automatically open the dialer so you can place the call instantly.
